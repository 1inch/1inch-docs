1inch Liquidity Sources

Liquidity sources play a crucial role in the functioning of the 1inch Network. To ensure optimal liquidity for Aggregation Protocol swaps, 1inch leverages a diverse set of decentralized exchanges (DEXes) and private liquidity providers. Constant efforts are made by 1inch to expand the pool of liquidity sources, thereby enhancing the efficiency of its algorithm.

Here is a comprehensive list of the chains/networks from which 1inch Network sources liquidity:
